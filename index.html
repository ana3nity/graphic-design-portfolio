Testimonial

Ana volunteered her services for a project as a graphic designer to Formed Families Forward (FFF), a nonprofit organization in northern VA that serves foster, adoptive and kinship families. She was professional, personable, creative, detail oriented and took the time to look at our materials so she could understand FFF's mission. It was a complex project with different materials done in both Spanish and English. Ana kept everything together and did so in a timely manner. She was a pleasure to work with and I look forward to the possibility of working with her again in the future.

Beth Behrin

Family Outreach and Support Director & Formed Families Forward
